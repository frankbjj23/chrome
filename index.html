Story

Each fitted starts with structured wool and hand-laid leather patchwork, cut to hold shape while adding tactile contrast that softens with wear.

The crown is finished with a custom sterling silver top button, cast in small runs and polished for a low-luster hardware note.

Production stays intentionally small so each drop can be refined by hand, inspected piece by piece, and released as a true limited capsule.

FAQ

When do orders ship?

Orders dispatch in 3-5 business days. Tracking is emailed once your package leaves the studio.

Are these hats authentic brand products?

No. These are custom, promotional concept pieces inspired by luxury streetwear aesthetics.

What materials are used?

Each hat uses structured wool blend body panels, genuine leather patchwork accents, and a custom sterling silver top button.

How should I choose my size?

Choose your standard fitted size. If you are between sizes, size up for a more relaxed fit.

Can I return a hat?

Returns are accepted within 14 days for unworn items with original tags attached.

How do I care for leather and silver details?

Spot clean with a soft cloth. Avoid soaking leather panels and store dry to preserve finish and structure.
